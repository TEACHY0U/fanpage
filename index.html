Chevelle Tribute Page

Bio

Chevelle was formed in 1995 by the Loeffler brothers. Pete plays lead vocals and guitar and Sam plays the drums and percussion. Matt Scott was also in the band but later left and was replaced by Joe Loeffler in 1996 who is the youngest brother of the group. The band since has had a few changes in lineups but they have continued to make music throughout the years.
The band has been classified as hard rock, alternative metal, post-grunge, nu metal, and indie rock. Chevelle's won the Hard Music Album and Hard Music Recorded Song at the GMA Dove Award in 2000.
